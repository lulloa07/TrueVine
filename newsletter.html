A Divine Connection

We Met Tahni Cullen — Author of Josiah's Fire.

One of the most unexpected blessings of this season: we were able to connect with Tahni Cullen, author of Josiah's Fire — the remarkable true story of her nonspeaking son Josiah, whose words on a letterboard revealed a profound inner life and faith that has touched readers around the world.

Her story is a living testimony of everything True Vine Mission Academy stands for: that nonspeaking does not mean unthinking, and that every child carries a voice worth hearing. We are grateful for her encouragement and excited about what this connection means for our work ahead.

"When the world finally listened to Josiah, what came out was wisdom, faith, and fire. Our students carry that same fire. We are building the school that will listen."

Founder Spotlight

Sunny Begins Her Practitioner Certification.

We are thrilled to share that our co-founder Sunny Talbert has traveled to Georgia to begin her certification process to become a Spellers Method Practitioner.

This is a major step forward for True Vine Mission Academy. Having a certified practitioner among our founding team means our students will be served by someone trained directly in the method that has unlocked thousands of nonspeaking voices around the world. Please keep Sunny in your prayers as she continues this important training.

Every step of this journey confirms what we already believed — that God is going before us, opening doors, and preparing the way for our students. Sunny's certification is one more answered prayer. — The True Vine Mission Team

About the School

We Are Building Something Different.

True Vine Mission Academy is a faith-based nonprofit school in Celina, Texas dedicated to serving nonspeaking and minimally speaking students through the Spellers Method — a communication approach rooted in presumed competence.

Opening February 2027. Enrollment inquiries welcome.
