🎙️ Podcast Interview

Podcast Interview — The Spellers Method in Action

Valeri's daughter Lora was underestimated in Special Ed — until she discovered "Underestimated" by JB Handley and connected with Dawn Marie Gaivin from the Spellers movie. Lora began spelling to communicate, finished her degree online, and is now taking college classes. Mom became a Spellers practitioner in San Diego.
